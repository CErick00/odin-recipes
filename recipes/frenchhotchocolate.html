French Hot Chocolate

Easy to make and delicious to drink. This is the perfect drink to come home to after a long day outside enjoying winters wonderland!

Here We Go!

Ingredients:

  • 1 1/2 cups milk
  • 1/2 cup cream
  • 2 t powdered sugar
  • 8 ounce chocolate - chopped
  • Whipped cream for topping
  1. In a medium saucepan over medium heat, whisk together the whole milk, heavy cream and powdered sugar until small bubbles appear around the edges. Do not allow the mixture to boil.
  2. Remove from saucepan from the heat and stir in the chopped chocolate until melted, returning the sauce to low heat if needed for the chocolate to melt completely.
  3. Serve warm, topped with lots of whipped cream.
